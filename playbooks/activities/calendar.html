Stories Strategy

Stories हे engagement चं engine आहे. Feed posts reach देतात, पण Stories loyalty build करतात.

Daily Story Ideas

  • सोमवार: "या आठवड्याचं schedule" — text story with batch timings
  • मंगळवार: Quick class BTS clip — 15 sec raw video
  • बुधवार: Poll — "कोणती activity try करायची?" / "Morning batch की Evening?"
  • गुरुवार: Student shoutout — performance clip or artwork photo
  • शुक्रवार: Fun quiz — "[ActivityName] trivia"
  • शनिवार: Weekend class highlights — photo/video dump
  • रविवार: Rest day tip — "घरी हे practice करा" / motivational quote

Story Highlights (Permanent)

  • "Classes" — सगळ्या activities चं overview
  • "Reviews" — Parent/student testimonials
  • "Events" — Past events, competitions
  • "Batches" — Current timings, fees, age groups
  • "FAQs" — Common questions answered
