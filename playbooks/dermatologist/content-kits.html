"Acne का होतं?" — Science Carousel

Format: Carousel (7 slides) • Pillar: Skin Science • Best Day: Thursday
Slides Script
Slide 1 — Cover / Hook
Headline: "Acne का होतं?"
Subtext: Science ने explain केलं — finally एकदा clear झालं
Visual: Simple acne illustration — no real pimple photos on cover
Background: Clean white, accent blue
Slide 2 — The 4 Factors
Headline: Acne = 4 factors एकत्र येतात
Body: 1️⃣ Excess sebum (oil)
2️⃣ Dead skin cells
3️⃣ Bacteria (C. acnes)
4️⃣ Inflammation
हे चारही एकत्र आले की pimple तयार होतो.
Visual: 4-factor diagram — circle illustration
Slide 3 — Sebum & Hormones
Headline: Sebum जास्त का होतो?
Body: Androgens (hormones) sebum glands stimulate करतात. Teenage years, periods, PCOS, stress — सगळे hormones trigger करतात. त्यामुळे acne teenage आणि hormonal phases मध्ये जास्त.
Visual: Hormone → sebum → pore clog flowchart
Slide 4 — Pore Clogging
Headline: Pore block कसं होतं?
Body: Dead skin cells normally shed होतात. पण sebum त्यांना stick करतो — pore block होतो. Blackhead आणि whitehead तयार होतात. हे open होईल तर blackhead, बंद राहिला तर whitehead.
Visual: Pore cross-section — open/closed diagram
Slide 5 — Bacteria & Inflammation
Headline: Bacteria आणि Inflammation
Body: Blocked pore मध्ये C. acnes bacteria वाढतो — immune system react करतो. Result: redness, swelling, पू — म्हणजेच pimple. जास्त squeeze केलं तर inflammation वाढतो — scar होतो.
Visual: Bacteria → immune response → pimple
Slide 6 — Common Triggers
Headline: Acne worse कधी होतो?
Body: ⚡ Stress — cortisol वाढतो, sebum वाढतो
⚡ Wrong products — comedogenic moisturizers
⚡ Diet — high glycemic foods (sugar, maida)
⚡ Phone touching face — bacteria transfer
⚡ Pillow case बदलत नाही — bacteria build-up
Visual: Trigger icons list
Slide 7 — CTA
Headline: Acne persistent आहे? Consultation घ्या.
Body: Acne hormonal, bacterial, किंवा lifestyle related असू शकतो. Root cause शोधल्याशिवाय treatment काम करत नाही. DM करा किंवा appointment book करा — link in bio.

Format: Carousel (8 slides) • Pillar: Treatment Explainer • Best Day: Wednesday

Visual: Clean clinical aesthetic — serum drops on surface
Tag: "TREATMENT EXPLAINER" badge
Slide 2 — What Is It
Headline: Chemical Peel म्हणजे काय?
Body: एक acid-based solution skin वर apply केलं जातं — controlled exfoliation होतं. Dead skin cells निघतात, नवीन cells regenerate होतात. Result: smoother, brighter, clearer skin.
Visual: Before peel → after peel skin illustration
Slide 3 — Types of Peels
Headline: 3 Types of Chemical Peels
Body: 🟡 Superficial: Glycolic, Lactic acid — gentle, glow, no downtime
🟠 Medium: TCA — acne scars, pigmentation — 5-7 days recovery
🔴 Deep: Phenol — severe scarring — doctor-only, hospital setting
Most clinic procedures = superficial/medium
Visual: 3-tier pyramid or cards
Slide 4 — Who Is It For
Headline: Chemical Peel कोणासाठी?
Body: ✅ Acne scars / post-acne marks
✅ Pigmentation, melasma
✅ Uneven skin tone
✅ Dull, tired-looking skin
✅ Fine lines (early aging)
❌ Active acne / open wounds (wait करा)
❌ Very sensitive / reactive skin (doctor decide करेल)
Slide 5 — What Happens During
Headline: Procedure कसं होतं?
Body: Step 1: Skin clean करतात
Step 2: Peel solution apply — 5-10 minutes
Step 3: Neutralize / remove
Step 4: Soothing moisturizer + SPF
Total time: 30-45 minutes
Sensation: Tingling, mild warmth — manageable
Visual: Step-by-step numbered infographic
Slide 6 — Aftercare
Headline: Peel नंतर काय करायचं?
Body: ✅ SPF 50+ mandatory — 2 आठवडे
✅ Gentle moisturizer, no actives 1 आठवडा
✅ Peeling skin scratch / peel करू नका
❌ No exfoliation, retinol, vitamin C for 1 week
❌ No direct sun exposure
❌ No heavy makeup 24 hours
Visual: Do/Don't two-column card
Slide 7 — Results & Sessions
Headline: Results कधी दिसतात?
Body: Glow: 3-5 days मध्ये. Pigmentation improvement: 3-4 sessions नंतर (monthly). Acne scars: 5-6 sessions (depends on depth). Maintenance: 3 महिन्यांनी एकदा.
