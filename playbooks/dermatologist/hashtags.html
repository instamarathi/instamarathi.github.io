Hashtag Strategy

Primary hashtags, 3 rotating sets (A/B/C), local hashtags, size mix rule. Weekly rotation system — Instagram ला fresh वाटतं.

Hashtag Strategy का important आहे? Hashtags हे discovery tool आहेत — non-followers पर्यंत reach होतो. Instagram चा algorithm हे पाहतो की कोणत्या hashtag community मध्ये account fit होतो. Wrong hashtags = wrong audience. Rotation केल्याने "shadowban" risk कमी होतो.

Hashtag Size — कशाचा काय अर्थ?

SizePosts CountStrategyRisk
Large Large 1M+ posts Broad reach — पण post लवकर दडतो High competition — hard to rank
Medium Medium 100K–1M posts Sweet spot — reach + visibility balance Moderate — best for most posts
Small Small / Niche Under 100K posts Less competition — niche audience finds you Low reach but highly targeted

Size Mix Rule

प्रत्येक post मध्ये 15–20 hashtags वापरा या ratio मध्ये:

Large 3–4 hashtags — broad reach साठी (#SkinCare, #DermatologistTips)
Medium 7–9 hashtags — core community reach (#AcneSkin, #SkinScience)
Small 4–6 hashtags — niche + local targeting (#PuneDermatologist, #MicroneedlingMumbai)

Primary Hashtags — Always Use (Core Set)

हे hashtags dermatologist account साठी core identity आहेत. प्रत्येक post मध्ये 5-6 primary hashtags rotate करत वापरा.

#SkinCareTips Large #DermatologistExplains Medium #SkinScience Medium #HealthySkin Large #SkinCareRoutine Large #AcneSkin Medium #SkinCareIndia Medium #DermatologyTips Medium #SkinExpert Medium #SkinHealth Medium

Rotating Sets — Week ने Change करा

खालील 3 sets weekly rotate करा — Set A first week, Set B second week, Set C third week, Set A again fourth week. Rotation केल्याने same hashtags वारंवार नाही येत — algorithm penalty नाही.

Set A — Skin Science + Education Focus

Week 1, Week 4, Week 7... Primary + Set A combined वापरा. Science-heavy posts आणि myth-busting साठी best.

#SkinMythBusting Small #SkincareFacts Medium #DermatologyFacts Small #SkincareScience Medium #MythVsFact Medium #SkinTips Large #AcneCare Medium #Pigmentation Medium #HairLoss Medium #DermatologistIndia Medium #SkincareEducation Small

Set B — Treatment + Clinic Focus

Week 2, Week 5, Week 8... Treatment explainer posts आणि before/after साठी best. Consultation intent वाले लोक या hashtags मध्ये असतात.

Week 3, Week 6, Week 9... Skincare routine posts, seasonal content, आणि quick tips साठी best. Broader lifestyle audience reach होतो.

Hashtag Best Practices

  • Caption मध्ये नाही — Comment मध्ये टाका: Clean caption राहतो. पहिला comment मध्ये hashtags — engagement same असतो, look professional.
  • Banned hashtags avoid करा: Occasionally check करा की हे hashtags Instagram ने restrict केले नाहीत. Check: hashtag page explore करा — "Recent Posts" नाही दिसल्यास banned आहे.
  • Niche hashtags वर focus: Large hashtags आणि competition जास्त — niche hashtags वर actual audience असतो.
  • Stories मध्ये 1-3 hashtags: Story reach थोडा वाढतो — #SkinCare किंवा local hashtag.
  • Monthly review: कोणते hashtags reach देत आहेत ते Instagram Insights मधून check करा. Top performing hashtags keep करा.

Client Hashtag Setup

Client च्या city, specialty (acne / hair / anti-aging focus) नुसार customize करा. Google Sheets मध्ये 3 sets save करा — copy-paste ready. प्रत्येक week ला comment ready ठेवा. Instagram Insights weekly check करा — "Impressions from Hashtags" track करा.
