Stories Strategy

Posts बरोबर daily stories पण important आहेत. Stories 24 तासात expire होतात पण engagement खूप जास्त मिळतो.

Daily Stories Plan

  • सोमवार: "आजचं quote" — motivational/educational quote
  • मंगळवार: "Did you know?" — शाळेबद्दल interesting fact
  • बुधवार: Poll/Quiz — subject related fun question
  • गुरुवार: Behind the scenes — classroom, lab, assembly
  • शुक्रवार: Student/Teacher shoutout
  • शनिवार: Study tip of the week
  • रविवार: Fun/engagement — "तुमची शाळेतली आठवण share करा"

Seasonal Notes

Admission Season Calendar

  • Schools (SSC/CBSE/ICSE): December-March — admission posts weekly, campus tours bi-weekly, results highlight
  • Colleges: May-June — admission rush, placement data, campus life reels daily
  • Pre-Schools: January-April — parent testimonials, activity videos, safety features
  • Coaching Classes: March-June (post-exam) + October-November (mid-year batch) — demo class offers, results showcase

Exam Season (Feb-April)

  • Study tips daily — carousel + stories
  • Motivational content increase
  • Parent guidance posts
  • Exam stress management tips

Results Season (May-June)

  • Results announcement — same day post
  • Topper spotlights — 3-4 posts
  • Alumni congratulation posts
  • Career guidance content spike
Pro Tip: Admission season मध्ये posting frequency वाढवा — दररोज 1 post + 3-5 stories. बाकी वेळी आठवड्यात 4-5 posts + daily 1-2 stories पुरेसे. Quality > quantity always.
