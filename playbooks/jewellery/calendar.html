30-Day Content Calendar

दिवस-दिवस posting plan — विषय, format, प्रकार, आणि hook. Designs न दाखवता 30 दिवस content. Client ला PDF म्हणून पाठवा.

Posting Time: सर्व feed posts 8–10 PM. Stories: morning 9 AM (gold rate update) आणि evening 7–8 PM (pre-post teaser). Sunday रात्री week preview story करा.

Pillar Key

P1: Styling Tips P2: Gold Rate / Investment P3: Festival / Wedding Guide P4: Craftsmanship BTS P5: Customer Moments P6: Care & Maintenance P7: Cultural Stories P8: Offers / New Arrivals
