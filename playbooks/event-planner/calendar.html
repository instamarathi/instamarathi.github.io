30-Day Content Calendar

दिवस-दिवस posting plan — विषय, format, प्रकार, आणि hook. Wedding season, birthday trends, festival hooks सगळं covered. Client ला PDF म्हणून पाठवा.

Posting Time: सर्व feed posts 7–9 PM (लोक relax करतात, events plan करतात). Stories: morning 9–10 AM (setup BTS) आणि evening 7–8 PM (pre-post teaser). Sunday रात्री week preview story करा.

Pillar Key

P1: Portfolio P2: Setup BTS P3: Theme Ideas P4: Budget Guide P5: Testimonials P6: Vendor Tips P7: Seasonal/Trending P8: DIY Tips
