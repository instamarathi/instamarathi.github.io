Draft ते Final — Complete Loop

Write → Validate → Fix → Validate again. हा loop एकदा समजला तर तुमची content quality permanently वर जाते. Dental carousel चा real walkthrough — सगळे stages.

Full Loop म्हणजे काय?

एखादा new creator एक prompt देतो, output आलं की publish करतो. Experienced creator loop run करतो: first draft → review → specific fixes → second draft → validation → final. हे 3-4 extra steps आहेत — पण result 3-4 पट better असतो. Client ला जेव्हा तुम्ही "मी हे validate करून आलोय, 5 checks केल्या आहेत" म्हणता, तेव्हा ते impressed होतात. हे तुम्हाला इतर freelancers पेक्षा different बनवतं.

Loop चे stages:

  1. Research — audience, fears, questions
  2. First Draft — initial script
  3. Self Review — weak parts identify करा
  4. Targeted Revision — specific fixes
  5. Validation — 5 checks (hook, voice, audience, comparison, facts)
  6. Final Fix — validation मधून आलेले issues fix करा
  7. Pre-Client Check — checklist complete करा
  8. Deliver — client ला send करा

Real Walkthrough — Dental Carousel "Root Canal Myths"

हा एक real example आहे — प्रत्येक stage कसा दिसतो ते बघा:

Stage 1 — Research Prompt

सुरुवात: client आहे Dr. Priya Smile Clinic, Pune. नवा topic: root canal myths. Research prompt:

Stage 1 — ResearchI'm creating Instagram content for a dentist in Pune. Topic: root canal treatment myths. Audience: urban Indians 25-45 who have been told they need a root canal but are delaying because of fear or myths. Tell me: 1. Top 5 myths about root canal treatment that patients in India believe 2. The real fear behind each myth (what emotion drives it — pain fear, cost fear, unknown fear?) 3. The actual fact that busts each myth — in simple, non-clinical language 4. For each myth, suggest one Instagram hook line that would stop someone scrolling (conversational, can mix Hindi/Marathi)

Output आलं. Top myths: "खूप दुखतं," "दात निघतो," "खूप वेळ लागतो," "एकदा केलं की वारंवार लागतो," "महागडं treatment आहे." Hooks आल्या. Research done.

Stage 2 — First Draft

Research च्याच conversation मध्ये draft मागवा:

Stage 2 — First DraftNow write a 6-slide carousel script using the myths and hooks above. Slide structure: - Slide 1: Hook (use the best hook from your list above — most emotional) - Slides 2-6: One myth per slide — myth (what people think) + truth (what actually happens) + one reassuring line - Final slide (Slide 7): CTA — warm invite to ask questions or book a consultation. Not pushy. Tone: warm, trustworthy, like a doctor friend explaining over chai. Not clinical. Include: Slide number, main text (max 3 short lines), sub-line (optional).

Draft आलं. पहिली reading: Slide 1 hook decent आहे. Slide 4 ("दात निघतो" myth) खूप long आहे. Slide 7 CTA थोडा stiff आहे. Identified: 2 fixes needed.

Stage 3 — Targeted Revision

Specific fixes — same conversation मध्ये:

Stage 3 — Targeted RevisionTwo fixes needed: 1. Slide 4 is too long and feels clinical. Rewrite it — shorter (max 2 lines main text + 1 sub-line). Keep the fact but make it sound like a friend reassuring, not a doctor explaining. 2. Slide 7 CTA feels stiff. Rewrite it to feel warmer and more human. Something like: invite them to DM their biggest dental fear. Promise a personal reply. No "Book now" energy. Keep all other slides exactly the same. Show me the revised Slide 4 and Slide 7 only.

Revised slides आल्या. Slide 4 आता concise आणि warm. Slide 7 CTA: "तुमची dental fear काय आहे? DM करा — personally reply करीन." Much better.

Stage 4 — Validation Run

Final draft तयार आहे. आता 5 validation checks:

  • Hook Check: Slide 1 hook "Root canal ऐकलं की घाबरतो — पण हे facts माहीत आहेत का?" — Score: Scroll-stop 4/5, Curiosity 4/5, Specificity 3/5. Specificity कमी आहे — LLM ने suggested: "2 वर्षांपासून dentist टाळतोयस? हे myths असतील कदाचित." Improved. Use केलं.
  • Brand Voice Check: एक line flagged: "Don't wait until it gets worse" — sounds slightly fear-based, off-tone. Replaced with: "जितक्या लवकर येशील, तितकं सोपं treatment."
  • Audience Relatability: LLM persona (30-year-old Pune resident avoiding dentist) said: "Slide 3 ला थोडा confusing वाटला — 'endodontic therapy' शब्द नको." Removed. Simple language kept.
  • Comparison: 2 hook versions compare केल्या. LLM ने original पेक्षा revised hook better rated केला — reasoning: "More specific ('2 years avoiding') triggers stronger identification."
  • Factual Check: All claims flagged as "High reliability." One note: "Mention that results vary — add 'consult your dentist' once." Added to Slide 7.

Stage 5 — Final Script

All fixes applied. Script now:

  • Slide 1: Improved hook (specific, emotional)
  • Slides 2-6: Clear myths busted, warm tone, no jargon
  • Slide 7: Human CTA + "consult your dentist" disclaimer

Total prompts used: 5 (research + draft + revision + 5 validations). Total time: ~25 minutes. Client-ready output.

Exercise — Bakery Carousel — Research ते Final Script

Niche: एक artisan bakery in Pune (custom cakes, birthday cakes, wedding cakes). Client: "Arya's Bakehouse." Tone: warm, celebratory, homemade-feel. Audience: women 25-40 who order custom cakes for family occasions.

Topic: "Custom cake order कसं करायचं — 5 steps" (educational carousel for first-time customers)

Complete these stages:

  1. Research: Research prompt — bakery audience च्या top questions (ordering custom cake, fear of getting it wrong, timeline, pricing). Hook suggestions.
  2. First Draft: 6-slide carousel script draft — hook + 4 steps + warm CTA.
  3. Review: Read it. Identify 1-2 weak slides. Note them down.
  4. Targeted Revision: Specific fixes prompt — exactly which slides, exactly what to change.
  5. Validation: Run all 5 validation checks — hook strength, brand voice, audience, comparison (if applicable), factual accuracy (no harmful claims for bakery, but check for misleading promises).
  6. Final Check: Pre-client checklist — tick every box.

हे सगळं एका LLM conversation मध्ये करा. End ला तुमच्याकडे एक complete, validated, client-ready carousel script असेल. हे deliverable आहे.
